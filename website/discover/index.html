Guia da Ilha do Sal

Tudo o que precisa de saber antes de vir

Everything you need to know before coming

Chegada
Getting there
Como Chegar ao Sal
How to Get to Sal
Voos directos de Lisboa, Londres, Amsterdam e Frankfurt. O Aeroporto Internacional Amilcar Cabral fica a 3km de Santa Maria. Transfer por taxi ou rent-a-car.
Direct flights from Lisbon, London, Amsterdam and Frankfurt. Amilcar Cabral International Airport is 3km from Santa Maria. Transfer by taxi or car rental.
Alojamento
Where to stay
Onde Ficar no Sal
Where to Stay in Sal
Santa Maria e a principal zona turistica com praias de areia branca. Espargos e a capital. Hoteis de 1 a 5 estrelas, pensoes familiares e bungalows frente ao mar.
Santa Maria is the main tourist area with white sand beaches. Espargos is the capital. Hotels from 1 to 5 stars, family guesthouses and seafront bungalows.
Actividades
What to do
O Que Fazer no Sal
What to Do in Sal
Kitesurf e windsurf em Ponta Preta, mergulho na Pedra de Lume, passeios de buggy pela ilha, salineiras e avistamento de tartarugas marinhas de Maio a Setembro.
Kitesurfing and windsurfing at Ponta Preta, diving at Pedra de Lume, buggy tours across the island, salt mines and sea turtle sightings from May to September.
